About Us

Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) MSRIT Bangalore is a Platform for the MSRIT students dedicated to development of multidimensional skills.

Vision

To spread the spirit of leadership, Entrepreneurship / Intrapreneurship, to nurture and encourage the expression of fresh, creative and innovative ideas and channelize the best of these to actual implementation.

Mission

To build a workculture among ourselves and in confidence and competency to be able to critically analyse our plan and provide constructive feedback that will help in the overall redesign and delivery of training.
