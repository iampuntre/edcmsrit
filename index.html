About Us

Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) MSRIT Bangalore is a Platform for the MSRIT students dedicated to development of multidimensional skills.

Vision

To spread the spirit of leadership, Entrepreneurship / Intrapreneurship, to nurture and encourage the expression of fresh, creative and innovative ideas and channelize the best of these to actual implementation.

Mission

To build a workculture among ourselves and in confidence and competency to be able to critically analyse our plan and provide constructive feedback that will help in the overall redesign and delivery of training.

Why EDC?

Entrepreneurship Development Cell basically aims at recognizing and developing soft skills of individuals. To be a successful entrepreneur it is important for an individual to be a leader, a team player and a risk taker. He should also be an excellent judge of opportunities and recognize the potential a market can offer.

Our Activities

EDC is involved in a range of activities from conducting workshops to talks. EDC MSRIT also has its annual EWEEK Program where the college promotes Entrepreneurship. Check out our activities!

Workshops

We have weekly workshops for EDC Members to grow their skills. Our workshops help in public speaking, group discussions, creavitity and lots more!

Talks
